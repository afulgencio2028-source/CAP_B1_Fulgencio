Best Work - Survey

Which Boba Drink You Should Get Based On Your Favorite Aniamls

This is my favorite and my best work. This is an example of my best work because I worked the hardest on this survey. I collected data from friends, family, and other students for this essay, which I really enjoyed seeing everyone’s preferences. I am proud of the way it turned out like the look of it and I also really enjoyed taking the survey. The most difficult part was making the chances of getting each boba drink as even as possible, but it was hard because from the data, most people preferred brown sugar milk tea. To make this survey, I had to learn how to use variables and nested if-else statements.
