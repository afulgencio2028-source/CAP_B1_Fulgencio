Growth - Game

Rainbow Unicorns

This game represents growth because it used a lot of things we learned earlier in the year. For example, variables, functions, and conditionals. This game also includes newer things to that unit like sprites. The hardest part was definitely coding the movement of the carrots and the rainbows. It was hard to make them come from the top at different times, so I had to make them all different speeds. Also, it was difficult to make the carrots and rainbows not overlap each other and I still cannot find a way to stop that. Despite the challenges, I think the game turned out fun and I worked really hard on it. If I could improve it, I would try to fix the bugs even though Code.org said there were not any bugs.
